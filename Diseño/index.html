Why just me?

Web Design is something I feel really passionate about, so I decided to do it alone, in order to learn more, and to push myself further. Besides, you just need one person to come with a great idea.

Problem & Solution

How do teenagers can make easy money? What do I do if I want for example a college education but I don't have the money? But I don't have a degree , how can I get a job? Our main problem is to move the economy, and the bussiness education among college/high school students. And what makes this idea unique? The fact that it involes fashion, whether you are interested in it or not, our lifes are surrounded by it. And I'm going to give you a solution. Vindemia, a website where you can sell your clothes and accessories. You take a picture of the piece, uploaded it and we tell you the amount of money you can sell it. After an agreement, its done! This is a way of promoting your style and gaining some money for it.

Impact

This isn't only an economical impact, we are talking about society and culture itself. Here in Mexico we are not used to buy secondhand clothes or accessories. We are so used to do everything that our neighbour country does (USA) that we don't want to learn from other countries. Example UK. Besides, this is a fun way to learn a bit of business and accounting, and like I said fashion.
